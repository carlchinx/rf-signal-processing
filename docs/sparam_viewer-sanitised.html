What this shows: A bandpass filter lets signals through in one frequency window and blocks everything else. S21 (transmission) measures how much signal passes port-to-port — 0 dB = perfect pass-through, lower = more blocked. S11 (return loss) shows how much is reflected back at the input — good design means low reflection in the passband. The four traces are individual production units — differences reveal manufacturing spread.
